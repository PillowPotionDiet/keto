## Recommended Meta Tags for Upsell/Downsell Pages

### Robots Meta Tag Strategy
For upsell/downsell pages, keep: `noindex, nofollow, nosnippet`
- Prevents duplicate content issues
- Keeps funnel pages out of search results
- Still allows social sharing via OG tags

### Open Graph Strategy
Even with noindex pages, OG tags are important for:
- Social media sharing
- Better link previews
- Professional appearance
